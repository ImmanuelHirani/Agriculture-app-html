About us

Jining Fenduni Foodstuff Co., Ltd. is established in 2005, which is a professional garlic producer and exporter. The factory is located in Jinxing County (The Hometown of Chinese Garlic), Jining City, Shandong Province, China. We have rich experience in growing, harvesting, selecting, processing, packing, refrigerating, transporting, and exporting. So we are able to provide our customers top quality garlics, competitive price, fast delivery and best service. Based on the advantages of origin, facing the international market, our company established our own production of planting base in Jinxiang County. We have 16 points of purchase, 18 cold storage with capacity of over 13,000 Metric Tons. The annual sale volume of our factory is up to 60,000 Metric Tons.
